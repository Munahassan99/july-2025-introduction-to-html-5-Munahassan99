About Luna & Bloom

At Luna & Bloom, we believe that every sip should be as nourishing as it is delicious. Our smoothies are handcrafted with the freshest fruits, vegetables, and superfoods — all sourced locally whenever possible.

Whether you’re starting your morning, grabbing a midday boost, or winding down after a workout, our blends are designed to refresh, energize, and delight.

Our Smoothie Menu

Tropical Sunrise

A bright mix of mango, pineapple, orange, and coconut water for the perfect sunny pick-me-up.

Green Glow

Spinach, kale, banana, avocado, and almond milk — a creamy green powerhouse full of nutrients.

Berry Bloom

Strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, and chia seeds blended into a vibrant antioxidant-rich treat.

Protein Punch

Banana, peanut butter, oats, cacao, and plant-based protein for the ultimate post-workout fuel.

Our Promise to You

Fresh and Inclusive

We cater to all — whether you need dairy-free, gluten-free, or nut-free options, we’ve got you covered. We label all allergens clearly and our team is trained to accommodate special dietary needs.

Accessible to All

Our shop is wheelchair accessible, menus are available in large print, and we use clear, descriptive labels for all products. Our website follows accessibility best practices to serve every customer equally.
